Giving back to the community
____________________
Beside working alongside agencies or together with clients, 
I’m co-organising a quarterly inspirational get together event for
digital creatives in Amsterdam called Ctrl A.

Doing this together with fellow rockstar freelance animator, Kabo Lam. Together we organise and host the event. Each time with two industry speakers, a live Q&A with the audience and networking.

Held at the amazing 18th floor of the A’DAM Toren, with a panorama view of Amsterdam. Thanks to our collaboration with creative member club A’DAM&Co. 

We’ve had already amazing speakers from BUCK, Woodwork, Kingdom of Something and more. With a goal to keep brining value to animators, motion designers and digital creatives alike.
